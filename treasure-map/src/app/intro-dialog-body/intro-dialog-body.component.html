The big day is here! Bill Cipher desperately needs your help. He has found a way to forge an Amulet of Protection using the Zodiac signs.

This will protect him from the people in Gravity Falls as he finishes his master plan, but he needs you to help him find all 10 Zodiac signs.

If you can help him forge this magical Amulet, maybe he can help you with a clue.... There is only one way to find out.


The Rules

Bill Cipher is searching everywhere to find out where these Zodiac Signs might be buried. He says he will temporarily give you COMPLETE control.


Walk around using ↑ ← ↓ → OR W A S D


As you are walking around, listen for sounds. As you get closer to burried treasure, the sounds will become faster and higher pitched.

Once you think you have found the location of some treaure, press Space Bar to dig it up and see if its a Zodiac Sign or something else.


After you find a Zodiac sign, a portal, , will appear.

Walk to the portal and press Space Bar so Bill can teleport to the next region where he believes a Zodiac sign could be found.

Don't forget to search for other treasures before teleporting away though! You never know what useful items can be discovered.


You can check your progress by pressing P.
